## 🌟 Key Features

### 📊 Comprehensive KPI Suite
Get instant visibility into market health with real-time tracking of:
- **Total Listings**: 1,064 records across major hubs.
- **Average Market Price**: Dynamically updated based on filters.
- **Usage Metrics**: Average kilometer driven trends.
- **Ownership Analysis**: Split by 1st, 2nd, and 3rd+ owners.
- **Quality Benchmarking**: Average quality scores across brands.

### 🔍 Advanced Multi-Level Slicing
Drill down into the data with precision using our interactive filter panel:
- **Brand & City**: Filter by 23 major brands and 11 Indian cities.
- **Condition Filters**: Model year, mileage ranges, and quality scores.
- **Specifications**: Fuel type (Petrol, Diesel, CNG, etc.) and Body styles (SUV, Hatchback, Sedan).

### 🗺️ City Explorer & Insights
- **Interactive Grid**: Visual city explorer to compare regional market variations.
- **AI-Driven Insights**: Curated "Market Intelligence Cards" highlighting volume vs. value, age decay, and premium trends.
